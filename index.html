ABOUT ME

Richard is an aspiring web developer. He possesses the necessary skills in areas of both front-end and back-end development. His goal is to keep building on those skills. He's passionate, and is determined to work with other web developers. Computer languages are always changing, and with it, so does technology. Richard is about creating fully functional and user-friendly applications. He wants to make use of his abilities to pursue and work towards solutions for the betterment of web-based applications, products and any team he is a part of.

Speech Share

My own personal application. It's design is to serve as a motivator for any individual. If you're feeling down and out, turn to "Speech Share" and reenergize. In it, you will find speeches that contain politics, sports and films. These selected speeches are imported via, my own seeded database. Also, to view those specific speeches, above them is a search bar that calls onto the YouTube API. Here, you can search for the seeded speeches, or look up any other speech.
